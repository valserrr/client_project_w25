First Generation at U of M

First Generation students

There are approximately 4,000 students at the University of Michigan that are the first generation in their family to be attending college. Being the first is nerv wracking in many ways but here at U-M there are many ways to explore and find any help you will need.

Find your community

There are useful networks and programs at U-M that can help you connect and thrive!

Am I a First Generation student?

At U-M students are considered first generation if neither parent or guardian has completed a 4-year college or university degree.
